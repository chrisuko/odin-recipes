Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This Nigerian-style fried rice is full of colorful vegetables, savory chicken, and perfectly seasoned rice that brings the taste of celebration to your table.

It’s perfect for parties or Sunday lunch, and it pairs wonderfully with grilled chicken or plantains.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, sweet corn)
  • 1/2 cup diced liver or chicken
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and seasoning cubes to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onion until fragrant.
  2. Add liver or chicken and stir-fry until cooked.
  3. Mix in vegetables, curry, and thyme; cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Add cooked rice, stir well, and season to taste.
  5. Cook for 5 more minutes while stirring continuously.
  6. Serve hot with fried plantain or grilled chicken.
