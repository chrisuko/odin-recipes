lasagna Recipes

Description

This lasagna recipe is a comforting classic layered with rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and perfectly seasoned minced meat. It’s baked to perfection for a golden, cheesy finish.

Whether you're hosting a family dinner or just craving something hearty, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned, then mix in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of sauce, then noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat the layers.
  6. Top with extra cheese and bake for 30–35 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
